"Save the State" Fundraiser

Update:

Auburn State Theater has received a grant from the USDA-RD for $15,300.00 that will be applied to further theater rennovations! Thank you to the USDA-RD for your generous contribution to our local, school-owned theater!

We're almost there! In less than one year, the Auburn State Theater has raised over $100,000 to restore one of the most popular entertainment venues in the community. Community members can now enjoy a new movie delivered from our HD Digital Projector. Watch movies in RealD 3D and High Frame Rate. We are also XBrite Certified with a new 7.1 Sound System. Sit back in our new seats and enjoy the show!

We're not done yet, though! We will wrap up the renovations with improvements to our lobby and concessions area! We appreciate the support from those who have made this a great success!
